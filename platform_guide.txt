
Platform Types Guide

1. Normal Platform (Green)
   - Regular platform with standard friction
   - Basic jumping and movement physics

2. Ice Platform (Light Blue)
   - Slippery surface with reduced friction
   - Makes it harder to stop or change direction
   - Requires more careful movement control

3. Bounce Platform (Pink)
   - Provides an extra high bounce when landed on
   - Great for reaching higher places
   - Automatically boosts jump height

4. Speed Platform (Yellow/Gold)
   - Temporarily increases player's maximum speed
   - Speed boost lasts for 1 second
   - Perfect for crossing large gaps
